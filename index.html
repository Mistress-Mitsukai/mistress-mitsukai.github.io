Top 5 Things that I Love to Do!

Life is definitely one of the greatest pkaygrounds that a human can be presented with. Sometimes, it is what the human chooses to do on this playground that defines them and their personalities. I feel like many of the things that I enjoy doing have something to do with different aspects of my personalities, since can be reserved, yet spontaneous at the same time.

1. Watching Videos

Ever since childhood, I began to pick up on other things and sayings from watching videos on YouTube, TV shows, and anime shows on CrunchyRoll and KissAnime. Not only to I find myself learning different lessons and strategies from watching such videos, but they provided a great source of entertainment. Also, I could watch television while being grounded from playing video games (becuase according to my logic back then, you couldn't play a TV).

2. Drawing

It took me a while to realize that I loved to draw, but it was a way I could freely express myself without having to worry about tripping over my words while atempting to communicate verbally with somebody. Eventually, after looking at simple, yet wonderfully expressed drawings that other poeple began to post on Wattpad, I decided that I wanted to share my drawings with everybody and slowly improve my own drawing style by drawing fanart and comics for other people.

3. Writing Stories

Something I've always wanted to do since I was a child was to write a novel and becomme a famous, well-respected author. Even though I'll admit that my writing was beyond terrible in elementary school, I improved by taking many extracurricular forces to the point where I could easily write characters and do analyses on other people's characters. I have tried writing many stories, but I have never been able to finsih one, due to me having a tendency to lose motivation easily.

4. Daydreaming

I'm not a really big fan of reality and the real world, so I tend to tune out whenever I feel upset or saddened in some way. Whenever I sleep or feel like daydreaming, I sort of enter my own universe, an alternate reality in which a near-utopia exists. There, I have "imaginary" friends that I go on adventures with and are always willing to listen to me no matter what, even if I'm the only one who can see or hear them. I've always considered daydreaming a way to get away from my life for a while and reside in bliss with beings who I can truly trust.

5. Reading Fanfiction

Ever since I accidentally discovered fanfiction when I was ten years old, I grew somewhat addicted to it, and would always search for a good one to read in my free time. Most of the fanfiction that I have read are romantic and deal with two characters being paired together, but I am also a big fan of comedic fanfiction, known as "crack-fics", if I ever need a good laugh to start the day.
